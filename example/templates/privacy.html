Introduction

BrewSync ("we", "our", "us") is a beer discovery and rating app. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS app and website.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username — chosen by you, displayed publicly alongside your ratings
  • Email address — used for account identification and login
  • Password — stored securely using Django's password hashing (not stored in plain text)

Social Login

If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive:

  • Your name (Apple only, if you choose to share it)
  • Your email address (or a private relay address if you use Apple's Hide My Email)
  • A unique identifier from the provider

We do not receive or store your Apple or Google password.

User-Generated Content

  • Beer ratings — your 1-5 star ratings, associated with your username

Automatically Collected

We do not collect analytics, device information, location data, or tracking identifiers. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and provide access to the app
  • To display your ratings alongside your username
  • To compute average beer ratings from all users
  • To sync your data between the website and the iOS app

Data Storage

Your data is stored in Couchbase Capella, a cloud database service hosted in the United States. Data is synced between the server and your device using Couchbase Lite (local on-device database) and Couchbase App Services (sync layer).

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your username and ratings are visible to other users of the app.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the app (user menu → Delete Account). This permanently removes:

  • Your user account
  • All your ratings
  • All authentication tokens

Account deletion is immediate and irreversible.

Security

We use industry-standard security practices including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • OAuth 2.0 / OpenID Connect for authentication
  • Hashed passwords (never stored in plain text)
  • Secure token storage in the iOS Keychain

Children's Privacy

BrewSync is not intended for children under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at edisimon@gmail.com.
